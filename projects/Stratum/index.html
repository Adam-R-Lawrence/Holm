Stratum

Stratum is a build-preparation and G-code generation toolkit designed for digital light processing (DLP) and stereolithography (SLA) photopolymerization printers. It consumes volumetric exposure plans exported from Torrentem simulations, slices them into exposure layers, and optimises lift trajectories for high-viscosity resins.

The toolchain includes a resin-aware slicing kernel, projection map calibration utilities, and an interactive preview that renders per-layer irradiance fields. Output profiles support machine-specific constraints such as peel timing, adaptive lift speeds, and oxygen inhibition zones.
