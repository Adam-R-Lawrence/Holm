Vadum

Vadum provides visualization tooling for results generated by Torrentem, helping inspect large simulation outputs and communicate numerical behaviour clearly.

Status
Visualization library
Use cases
Field inspection, residual tracking, time-series review
Audience
Research debugging, analysis, and presentation

Purpose

Make solver output easier to inspect during method development, with emphasis on diagnostics and state comparison rather than presentation graphics.

Methods and Tools

  • Readers for large field outputs and time-dependent simulation state.
  • Views for residuals, field values, derived quantities, and run metadata.
  • Interfaces designed around research debugging and reproducible review.

Current Limitations

Vadum is coupled to the evolving Torrentem output format and remains a research support tool rather than a general visualization product.

Related Tools

Vadum sits downstream of Torrentem and can be used to evaluate cases prepared through Ostium or photopolymerization runs connected to Stratum.
